About me

Tabai Ala Eddin

I have always been driven by creating something out of nothing. When I was younger, I started painting, then I found the digital world where everything was possible to create! I started creating everything I could: logos, flayers , other digital designs and websites. Today I mainly create different websites, graphic designs and logos, and also work with web programming both front-end and back-end, especially with Symfony. I have a broad knowledge base and can therefore help with many different kinds of projects. I started my web journey early on my own but have later also studied web technology at the university to refine, broaden and deepen my knowledge. When I create websites, I primarily work with WordPress just because it is such a fantastic and flexible tool that is constantly evolving with the times. Of course, I also work with techniques such as HTML and CSS and i can develop applications based on JS framworks or JEE but I prefer to create new websites with Symfony.
